Abstract Art Extension is your chance to express yourself through a variety of cutting-edge art techniques, and using different materials and styles. We meet once a week to share observations and artwork, with the option to stay afterwards to work on your pieces. Abstract Arts is perfect for anyone who takes art as a subject and wants to try something different to their regular style, or anyone who has an interest in trying something new, regardless of previous art experience.
