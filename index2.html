What is Extension?

Extension is a programme that anyone at PNGHS can participate in. Students join Extension groups, write trip reports, and earn Extension bars. It is also known as the PNGHS Gifted and Talented Programme, although it is open to anyone. Students join voluntarily or can be nominated by a teacher. There are x number of groups, run by students and staff, along with sub-programs such as CREST. Students do not need to participate in Extension to join groups, but those wishing to join should sign up using the button above, and schedule a meeting with Dr. Meikle.
