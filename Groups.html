Extension Groups

Any student can join any group, although some may have specific requirements. The groups are run by teachers or students, with something to suit almost any interest. And if you can't find the group you really want, talk to Dr. Meikle and start one! Although students can join groups without participating in the full Extension programme, to gain an Extension bar you will need to participate fully in at least two Extension groups. (Or lead one.)
